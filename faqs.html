Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering from The Minums.

Orders

How do I place an order?
You can place an order through our website by selecting your preferred drinks and adding them to the cart.
Can I customize my drink?
No, we do not offer customization options. Only cup size selection is available.

Payment

What payment methods are accepted?
We accept online banking, e-wallets, and cash for self-pickup orders.
Is payment secure?
Yes, all payments are processed securely to protect customer information.

Delivery & Pickup

Do you offer delivery?
Yes, delivery is available for selected areas near our store.
How long does delivery take?
Estimated delivery time is between 30 to 60 minutes, depending on order volume and location.
Can I choose self-pickup?
Yes, you may choose self-pickup at our store during operating hours.

Issues & Support

Can I cancel my order?
Orders cannot be cancelled once the preparation process has started.
What if I receive the wrong order?
Please contact us immediately and we will assist you accordingly.
